Course Overview

Essential data protection and privacy compliance training

POPI Act designed to help students understand the importance of protecting personal information and how to correctly give informed consent in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). Though the POPI Act is a South African law, it is highly relevant for students engaging with South African educational institutions, research projects, or online platforms that collect personal data.

Understanding POPIA

Comprehensive legal framework for data protection

The Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) is a comprehensive legal framework designed to safeguard individuals' personal information and regulate how organisations collect, store, process, and share this data. The Act aims to ensure that personal information is handled with care, respecting privacy and confidentiality.

Key Objectives

  • Promote transparency in data handling
  • Build trust between data subjects and processors
  • Ensure security in personal data handling
  • Provide individuals with control over their data

Core Principles

  • Lawful processing of personal data
  • Obtaining proper consent
  • Ensuring data accuracy
  • Implementing adequate security measures
