What This Course About?

Essential training in basic first aid practices

This course offers essential training in basic first aid practices, focusing on immediate and effective response to a variety of medical emergencies. You will learn how to assess situations, provide CPR, manage wounds, burns, fractures, and respond to shock or unconsciousness. With a strong focus on workplace and everyday safety, this course empowers individuals to act confidently and potentially save lives before professional help arrives.

Why Take This Course?

Emergencies can happen anytime, anywhere. This course provides the practical knowledge and hands-on techniques needed to respond quickly and responsibly, whether at work, home, or in public. It's ideal for anyone looking to enhance their personal safety and workplace readiness.

Skills You'll Gain

  • Assess emergency situations and respond appropriately
  • Perform CPR and other life-saving techniques
  • Treat wounds, burns, and fractures with confidence
  • Support colleagues and family in times of medical need
  • Meet workplace health and safety requirements
