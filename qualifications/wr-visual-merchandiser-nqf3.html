Qualification Overview

The Occupational Certificate: Visual Merchandiser (NQF Level 3) provides specialized knowledge and skills for creating effective retail displays and visual presentations. This qualification is designed for individuals seeking positions in visual merchandising, retail marketing, and store presentation roles.

Students will gain creative and technical skills in developing visual merchandising plans, implementing displays, maintaining visual standards, and evaluating the effectiveness of retail presentations.

Learning Modules

Module 1: Developing Visual Merchandising Plans (6 Credits)

  • • Concepts and principles of developing visual merchandising plans (4 Credits)
  • • Develop and prepare an action plan for the implementation of visual merchandising plans/displays/windows (2 Credits)

Module 2: Implementing Visual Merchandising Plans (3 Credits)

  • • Concept and principles of implementing visual merchandising plans/action plans (3 Credits)

Module 3: Maintain and Evaluate Visual Merchandising Plans (9 Credits)

  • • Concepts and principles of maintaining and evaluating visual merchandising displays (2 Credits)
  • • Maintain and evaluate visual merchandising displays (2 Credits)
  • • Processes and procedures for maintaining and evaluating visual merchandising displays (5 Credits)

Module 4: Visual Merchandising and Displays (12 Credits)

  • • Dress the display/window (2 Credits)
  • • Processes and procedures for setting up visual merchandising displays (10 Credits)
