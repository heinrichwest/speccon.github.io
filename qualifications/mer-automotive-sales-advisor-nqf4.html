Occupational Certificate: Automotive Sales Advisor

Master the art of automotive sales with comprehensive training in vehicle knowledge, customer relations, sales techniques, and industry best practices to excel as a professional automotive sales advisor.

Qualification Overview

The purpose of this qualification is to prepare a learner to function as an Automotive Sales Advisor who sells vehicles, accessories, spare parts, and services in automotive retail and wholesale environments.

Key Capabilities

Upon completion of this qualification, learners will be able to:

  • Sell automotive vehicles to clients
  • Sell automotive vehicle parts and accessories
  • Sell automotive vehicle services
  • Provide professional customer service in automotive retail environments
  • Handle stock control and inventory management
  • Understand vehicle finance processes

Entry Requirements

  • Minimum NQF Level 3 qualification in a related automotive sales industry field

Career Opportunities

This qualification opens doors to various automotive sales opportunities including:

Senior Sales Advisor
Used Car Sales Specialist
Fleet Sales
Sales Management

Assessment

This qualification requires external integrated summative assessment through a QCTO accredited assessment center.

Additional Information

This qualification is part of the Quality Council for Trades and Occupations (QCTO) framework and is designed to meet the needs of the automotive retail industry in South Africa. The combination of theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and work experience ensures that learners are fully prepared for the demands of working as an Automotive Sales Advisor in various automotive retail and wholesale settings.
