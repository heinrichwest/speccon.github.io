Qualification Overview

The Design Thinking Practitioner qualification provides comprehensive training in human-centered design methodologies, creative problem-solving, and innovation processes. This program equips learners with the skills to understand user needs, generate innovative solutions, and implement design thinking approaches across various business contexts and industries.

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this qualification, learners will be able to:

  • Apply design thinking methodologies to solve complex problems
  • Conduct user research and develop empathy for target audiences
  • Facilitate ideation sessions and collaborative workshops
  • Create prototypes and test solutions with users
  • Implement iterative design processes and feedback loops
  • Improve customer experience and product design outcomes

Modules and Unit Standards

Module 1: Design Thinking Fundamentals and Benefits (10 credits)

  • • Design Thinking Fundamentals (NQF Level 4, 4 credits)
  • • Benefits of Design Thinking (NQF Level 4, 2 credits)
  • • 4IR and Future Skills (NQF Level 4, 4 credits)

Module 2: Positive Psychology and Human Centered Innovation (12 credits)

  • • Fundamentals of Positive Psychology for Design Thinking (NQF Level 4, 4 credits)
  • • Human Centered Innovation (NQF Level 4, 4 credits)
  • • Collaboration (NQF Level 4, 4 credits)

Module 3: Design Thinking Research, Governance and Ethics (20 credits)

  • • Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced Computer Technology and Utilisation (Level 3, 2 credits)
  • • Introduction to Design Thinking Governance, Legislation and Ethics (NQF Level 4, 2 credits)
  • • Research to Inform Design Thinking Processes (NQF Level 4, 4 credits)
  • • Conduct Applied Ethnographic Research (Level 4, 8 credits)
  • • Applied Ethnographic Research (Level 4, 4 credits)

Module 4: Innovation in the Workplace (28 credits)

  • • Apply Design Thinking for Innovation Principles and Methodologies (Level 4, 24 credits)
  • • Apply Ethics and Effectiveness in the Workplace (Level 4, 4 credits)

Module 5: Project Management and Design (30 credits)

  • • Introduction to Project Management for Design Thinking (NQF Level 5, 4 credits)
  • • Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced Computer Technology and Utilisation (Level 3, 2 credits)
  • • Creative Innovation Solution Design (Level 4, 24 credits)
