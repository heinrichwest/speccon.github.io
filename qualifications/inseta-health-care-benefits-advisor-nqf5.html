Qualification Overview

This Health Care Benefits Advisor qualification provides specialized training in health care benefits advisory services, medical aid programs, and employee health benefits planning. The program combines regulatory knowledge with practical skills needed to provide expert health benefits consultation.

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this qualification, learners will be able to:

  • Analyze health care benefits needs and requirements
  • Evaluate medical aid schemes and health insurance options
  • Provide advice on health care benefit programmes
  • Support implementation of health care benefit solutions
  • Apply regulatory frameworks for health care benefits
  • Understand investment advisory principles in health benefits

Career Opportunities

This qualification opens doors to specialized health benefits roles including:

Health Care Benefits Consultant
Medical Aid Advisor
Employee Benefits Specialist
Benefits Administration Manager

Core Modules

Module 1: Introduction to Investment Advisory Industry

  • • Investment Advisory Industry Overview (13 credits)
  • • Understanding the role of investment advisory in health benefits
  • • Regulatory environment and compliance frameworks
  • • Professional ethics and standards in advisory services
  • • Industry trends and developments in health benefits

Module 2: Principles of Providing Advice

  • • Regulatory Requirements for Health Benefits Advice (17 credits)
  • • Client consultation and needs assessment techniques
  • • Advisory process and documentation standards
  • • Risk management in health benefits advisory
  • • Professional conduct and client relationship management

Module 3: Health Care Benefits Specialization

  • • Health Care Benefits Advisory Services (15 credits)
  • • Analyze Health Care Benefits Needs (17 credits)
  • • Provide Health Care Benefit Programme Advice (26 credits)
  • • Participate in Health Care Benefit Implementation (14 credits)
  • • Medical aid scheme evaluation and comparison
  • • Employee health benefits optimization strategies
