Qualification Overview

The Quality Assurer qualification provides comprehensive training in quality management systems, process improvement, and quality assurance activities. This program prepares professionals to ensure compliance with standards and regulatory requirements while managing quality assurance processes across various business environments.

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this qualification, learners will be able to:

  • Implement and maintain quality management systems effectively
  • Conduct quality assurance activities to ensure standards compliance
  • Manage quality assurance departments and business units
  • Conduct internal audits and risk profiling assessments
  • Analyze data and implement process improvements
  • Manage documentation and records within quality processes
