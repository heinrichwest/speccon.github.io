Qualification Overview

The National Certificate: Animal Production (NQF Level 4) is an advanced qualification designed for professionals seeking management roles in animal agriculture. This comprehensive program combines technical expertise with business management skills.

Students will master advanced animal husbandry techniques, production management systems, business planning, and leadership skills. The qualification prepares graduates for senior positions in livestock operations, farm management, and agricultural enterprises.

This qualification provides a pathway to higher education opportunities and positions graduates for advanced roles in the agricultural industry.
