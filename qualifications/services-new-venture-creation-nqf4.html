Qualification Overview

This New Venture Creation qualification provides comprehensive training in entrepreneurship, business planning, and startup management. The program combines theoretical business knowledge with practical skills needed to conceptualize, launch, and manage successful new ventures across various industries and business contexts.

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this qualification, learners will be able to:

  • Develop comprehensive business plans and strategies
  • Conduct market research and feasibility studies
  • Manage startup operations and business processes
  • Secure funding and manage financial resources
  • Implement marketing and sales strategies
  • Navigate legal and regulatory requirements

Core Modules

Module 1: Entrepreneurship Fundamentals

  • • Entrepreneurial mindset and opportunity recognition
  • • Innovation and creativity in business
  • • Risk assessment and management
  • • Entrepreneurial leadership and decision-making

Module 2: Business Planning and Strategy

  • • Business model development and validation
  • • Market research and competitive analysis
  • • Strategic planning and goal setting
  • • Business plan creation and presentation

Module 3: Financial Management

  • • Financial planning and budgeting
  • • Funding sources and investment strategies
  • • Cash flow management and financial controls
  • • Financial reporting and analysis

Module 4: Marketing and Sales

  • • Marketing strategy and brand development
  • • Digital marketing and social media
  • • Sales processes and customer acquisition
  • • Customer relationship management

Module 5: Operations and Legal Framework

  • • Business operations and process design
  • • Legal structures and regulatory compliance
  • • Intellectual property and contracts
  • • Quality management and continuous improvement
