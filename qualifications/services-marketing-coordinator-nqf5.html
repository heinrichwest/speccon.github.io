Qualification Overview

This Marketing Coordinator qualification provides comprehensive training in marketing strategy, digital marketing, brand management, and campaign coordination. The program combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills needed to develop, implement, and evaluate marketing initiatives across various industries and business contexts.

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this qualification, learners will be able to:

  • Develop and implement comprehensive marketing strategies
  • Coordinate digital marketing campaigns across multiple channels
  • Manage brand identity and marketing communications
  • Conduct market research and analyze consumer behavior
  • Plan and execute marketing events and promotions
  • Measure and evaluate marketing campaign effectiveness

Core Modules

Module 1: Marketing Strategy

  • • Marketing principles and strategic planning
  • • Market segmentation and targeting strategies
  • • Competitive analysis and positioning
  • • Marketing mix optimization (4Ps)

Module 2: Digital Marketing

  • • Social media marketing and content creation
  • • Search engine optimization (SEO) and SEM
  • • Email marketing and automation
  • • Digital advertising and PPC campaigns

Module 3: Brand Management

  • • Brand identity and positioning strategies
  • • Brand communication and messaging
  • • Brand equity measurement and management
  • • Corporate identity and visual standards

Module 4: Market Research

  • • Research methodology and data collection
  • • Consumer behavior analysis
  • • Market trends and forecasting
  • • Survey design and data interpretation

Module 5: Campaign Management

  • • Campaign planning and execution
  • • Budget management and resource allocation
  • • Performance measurement and ROI analysis
  • • Cross-channel campaign coordination
