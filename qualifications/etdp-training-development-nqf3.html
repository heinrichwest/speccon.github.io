Learning Outcomes

Training Design and Planning

Design and plan training interventions based on identified learning needs and organizational objectives.

Training Delivery

Facilitate learning sessions using various training methods and techniques to engage different learning styles.

Assessment and Evaluation

Conduct assessments and evaluate training effectiveness to ensure learning objectives are met.

Adult Learning Principles

Apply adult learning principles and theories to create effective learning environments.

Workplace Integration

Integrate training with workplace activities and support learner development in practical settings.

Core Modules

Fundamentals of Training

Introduction to training concepts, learning theories, and training methodologies.

Training Design

Systematic approach to designing effective training programs and materials.

Facilitation Skills

Developing presentation and facilitation skills for effective training delivery.

Assessment Methods

Various assessment techniques and evaluation methods for measuring learning outcomes.

Workplace Learning

Supporting learning in workplace environments and practical application of skills.

Communication Skills

Effective communication techniques for training and development contexts.
