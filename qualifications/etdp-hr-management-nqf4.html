Human Resource Management

NQF Level 4 • ETDP SETA

This comprehensive qualification covers all aspects of human resource management, from recruitment and selection to performance management and employee relations. Students will develop strategic HR skills and gain practical experience in managing human capital effectively.

240
Credits
18
Months Duration
4
NQF Level

Learning Outcomes

Recruitment and Selection

Design and implement effective recruitment strategies and conduct fair selection processes.

Performance Management

Develop and manage performance management systems to optimize employee productivity.

Employee Relations

Handle employee relations, disciplinary procedures, and conflict resolution effectively.

HR Strategy and Planning

Develop HR strategies aligned with business objectives and organizational goals.

Employment Law and Compliance

Apply employment legislation and ensure organizational compliance with labor laws.

Core Modules

Strategic HR Management

HR strategy, workforce planning, and organizational development.

Recruitment & Selection

Talent acquisition, interviewing techniques, and selection methods.

Performance systems, appraisals, and productivity improvement.

Employee relations, grievance handling, and labor negotiations.

Learning strategies, skills development, and career planning.

Labor legislation, compliance, and legal risk management.
