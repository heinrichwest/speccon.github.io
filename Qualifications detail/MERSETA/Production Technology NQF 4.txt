QUALIFICATION OVERVIEW
======================

Purpose:
This qualification provides learners with the range of learning and skills required to
be able to perform a series of activities to support manufacturing, engineering, and
technology processes. Learners will acquire a range of skills in the identification of
production parameters in manufacturing, engineering, and technology industries, and
basic strategies to achieve them.

ENTRY REQUIREMENTS
==================
Grade 11 / Computer Literacy

MODULES AND UNITS
=================

Module 1: Communication
- Accommodate audience and context needs in oral/signed communication
- Interpret and use information from texts
- Use language and communication in occupational learning programmes
- Write/present/sign texts for a range of communicative contexts
- Engage in sustained oral/signed communication and evaluate spoken/signed texts
- Read/view, analyse and respond to a variety of texts
- Use language and communication in occupational learning programmes
- Write/present/sign for a wide range of contexts

Module 2: Mathematical Literacy
- Apply knowledge of statistics and probability to critically interrogate and effectively communicate findings on       life-related problems
- Represent,  analyse, and calculate shape and motion in 2-and 3-dimensional space in different contexts
- Use mathematics to investigate and monitor the financial aspects of personal, business, national and  international issues

Module 3: Manage Finance
- Participate in the estimation and preparation of the cost budget for a project or sub-project and monitor and  control actual cost against the budget
- Interpret basic financial statements

Module 4: Monitor Team Effectiveness
- Induct a member into a team
- Maintain records for a team
- Conduct a structured meeting
- Analyse leadership and related theories in a work context
- Apply efficient time management to the work of a department/division/section
- Empower team members through recognising strengths, encouraging participation in decision making, and delegating tasks
- Demonstrate a basic understanding of the Primary labour legislation that impacts a business unit

Module 5: Project Planning
- Conduct project documentation management to support project processes
- Work as a project team member

Module 6: Orientation of the Production Environment
- Monitor the machining process, interpret statistical process control charts and rectify production problems
- Coordinate the improvement of productivity within a functional unit
- Formulate and implement an action plan to improve productivity within an organisational unit
- Monitor and control quality control practices in a manufacturing/engineering environment
- Schedule and monitor production
- Solve operational problems in a manufacturing/assembly context

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES
====================
Production Technologist
Supervisor
Manager
Team Leader
Quality Controller

EXIT LEVEL OUTCOME
======================
On achieving this qualification, the learner will be able to:
- Measure, control and improve factors influencing production
- Contribute to budgeting processes in an operational unit to optimise resources
- Solve operational problems in a production process
- Promote, implement and maintain procedures that support quality assurance and control
