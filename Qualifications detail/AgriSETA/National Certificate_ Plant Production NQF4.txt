QUALIFICATION OVERVIEW
======================

This qualification is designed to enable supervisors to progress towards junior farm manager positions in plant production. The qualification prepares learners to manage supervisors and working teams in agricultural processes while taking full responsibility for the quality and quantity of outputs in plant production operations.

Key objectives of this qualification include:
• Managing supervisors and coordinating working teams in plant production processes
• Taking responsibility for quality and quantity of outputs in agricultural operations
• Gaining competency in specialized plant production sub-fields including vegetables, fruit production, hydroponics, organic production, permaculture, agronomy, and horticulture
• Making responsible decisions based on agri-business principles and agricultural management practices
• Overseeing implementation of procedures and ensuring compliance with industry standards
• Developing advanced management and leadership skills in plant production environments
• Understanding farm operations across multiple plant production categories
• Implementing quality control, safety, and environmental management systems
• Preparing for higher management and technical production practices at NQF Level 5

This qualification positions learners for higher management roles and allows horizontal movement between different plant production sub-fields. It also supports recognition of prior learning and accommodates learners from diverse backgrounds including farm operators, school leavers, and those with practical experience. The qualification has been replaced by the Occupational Certificate: Horticultural Farm Foreman (ID: 101871) for those seeking current credentials.

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES
====================

Upon successful completion of this qualification, learners can pursue the following career paths:

• Junior Farm Manager in plant production
• Farm Manager overseeing plant production operations
• Agricultural Supervisor managing teams and processes
• Production Manager in various plant production sectors:
  - Vegetables
  - Fruit production
  - Hydroponics
  - Organic production
  - Permaculture
  - Agronomy
  - Horticulture
• Agricultural Operations Coordinator
• Quality Control Manager in plant production
• Horticultural Farm Foreman

This qualification also provides opportunities for:
• Progression to higher agricultural qualifications at NQF Level 5
• Advancement to senior management and executive positions in agriculture
• Horizontal movement between different plant production specializations
• Leadership positions in agricultural enterprises and cooperatives
• Participation in local, national, and international agricultural markets
• Development of farm management expertise and business planning skills
• Transition to Occupational Certificate: Horticultural Farm Foreman for updated credentials
