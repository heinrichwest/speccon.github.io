Our Story Since 2013

From humble beginnings to becoming South Africa's leading learning solutions provider

SpecCon was established in 2013 and has grown remarkably from just 1 employee to over 150 employees. What makes us truly proud is that more than 50% of our current employees came from our own learnerships - and many of them are now thriving in management positions today.

We've transformed from an Excel training company to South Africa's leading learnerships, QCTO, learning management system and online training company. Today, we operate from 6 offices across South Africa, making quality education accessible nationwide.
